# Chapter 1 --- Vision & Product

## 1.1 Executive Summary

OneDate is an AI-powered date planning platform designed to help couples
and individuals create memorable experiences instead of simply finding
places to eat.

Unlike traditional recommendation or reservation applications, OneDate
combines artificial intelligence, location services, weather data, local
events, user preferences, and relationship insights to generate complete
date experiences tailored to each user's personality and situation.

The application aims to remove the stress of planning while increasing
the quality and uniqueness of shared experiences.

OneDate is not a dating platform.

It does not help users find partners.

Instead, it helps people who already know each other create meaningful
memories together.

The long-term vision is to become the world's leading AI companion for
planning experiences and preserving memories.

## 1.4 Problem Statement

Planning a date seems simple but often requires multiple different
applications.

Users commonly need to:

-   Search Google Maps
-   Compare restaurants
-   Check weather forecasts
-   Search for nearby events
-   Find parking
-   Look for opening hours
-   Compare prices
-   Plan routes
-   Search Spotify playlists
-   Think of conversation topics
-   Estimate budgets

This process is fragmented across many services and often takes more
time than the date itself.

Additionally, recommendations are usually generic.

Popular restaurants are repeatedly suggested while unique local places
remain undiscovered.

Existing solutions also stop once the reservation has been made.

They rarely help users before, during, or after the actual experience.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1.5 Proposed Solution

OneDate combines all planning tasks into one intelligent platform.

Instead of recommending individual places, the application generates
complete date experiences.

Example:

``` text
18:00 Hidden Italian Restaurant
↓
19:45 Walk through the botanical garden
↓
20:30 Live Jazz Event
↓
22:00 Cocktail Bar
↓
22:45 Sunset viewpoint
↓
Memory Book
```

The application continuously considers:

-   Budget
-   Location
-   Transportation
-   Weather
-   User preferences
-   Food preferences
-   Date style
-   Opening hours
-   Travel time
-   Hidden gems
-   Events

The result is a complete experience instead of isolated recommendations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1.6 Product Philosophy

### 1. Personal over Popular

Recommendations should prioritize places that genuinely fit the user's
preferences. Whenever possible, local businesses and hidden gems should
be favored over global chains.

### 2. Experiences over Reservations

Restaurants are only one part of a successful date. Every plan should
combine multiple experiences such as cafés, museums, walks, live events,
viewpoints, dessert, games and cultural attractions.

### 3. Simplicity over Complexity

Planning should require only a few minutes. AI should make intelligent
assumptions whenever appropriate.

### 4. Memories over Transactions

OneDate continues after the date. Photos, notes, ratings, playlists and
personal memories become part of a shared relationship timeline.

### 5. Continuous Learning

The AI continuously improves from visited places, liked restaurants,
disliked activities, favorite cuisines and seasonal preferences.
